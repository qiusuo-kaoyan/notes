### 🔍 逐句像素级诊断

#### **第一句（定轮廓）**
> **你的原文：** Laid out in front of us is a cartoon concerning the improvement of public facilities.
*   **批改：** **完美！**

#### **第二句（描动态）**
> **你的原文：** Exercising energetically, in the public fitness facilities several residents are enjoying the benefit in the new park.
*   **问题 1（语序）：** `in the public fitness facilities` 插入在分词状语和主语之间，显得句子很碎。
*   **问题 2（时态）：** 虽然用进行时 `are enjoying` 描述图画没问题，但考研作文推荐多用一般现在时 `enjoy`，显得更客观稳重。
*   **修改建议：** 把地点状语挪到合适的位置，或者合并。
*   **🟢 升级版：** **Exercising energetically**, several residents **enjoy** the convenience of the public fitness facilities in the new park.

#### **第三句（挖细节·致命伤集中区 🚨）**
> **你的原文：** With an air of satisfaction, a young boy **are** prai**s**ing the new **build** park near his house.
*   **问题 1（主谓一致·致命伤）：** `a young boy` 是单数，谓语动词用了 `are`！这是绝对不能犯的低级错误，阅卷老师看到这里会直接扣印象分。
    *   🔴 `a young boy are` → 🟢 `a young boy is` 或 `a young boy praises`
*   **问题 2（词性错误）：** `build` 是动词，不能直接放在名词前做形容词。这里想表达“新建的”，应该用过去分词或副词+分词。
    *   🔴 `new build park` → 🟢 `newly built park` 或 `new park`
*   **问题 3（拼写）：** `praiseing` 多了个 `e`（虽然手写不太明显，但要注意 `praising`）。
*   **🟢 升级版：** **With an air of satisfaction**, a young boy **praises** the **newly built** park near his house.

#### **第四句（一句话概括图表）**
> **你的原文：** Meanwhile, the bar chart shows the **increasing** number of **build** parks from 406 in 2020 to 670 in 2022.
*   **亮点：** 你加上了 `increasing`，非常好！不仅描述了数据，还点出了趋势。
*   **问题（重复错误）：** 又出现了 `build parks`。
    *   🔴 `build parks` → 🟢 `parks` (前面已经说过 newly built 了，这里直接说 parks 即可) 或 `urban parks`。
*   **🟢 升级版：** Meanwhile, the bar chart shows the **increasing** number of **parks** in a city from 406 in 2020 to 670 in 2022.
