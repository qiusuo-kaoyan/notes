### 🔍 逐句像素级诊断

#### **第一句（定轮廓）**
> **你的原文：** Laid out in front of us is a **thought-provoking** cartoon concerning two different attitudes towards the same **setback**.
*   **💡 亮点 1：** 你加了 `thought-provoking`（发人深省的），这是一个非常棒的形容词，瞬间提升了句子的格调！
*   **💡 亮点 2：** `concerning` 后面的总结太精准了！`two different attitudes towards the same setback`（面对同一挫折的两种不同态度），这比单纯描写“两个人看瓶子”要高级得多，直接点题！
*   **批改：** **完美！**

#### **第二句（描动态）**
> **你的原文：** **Staring at** the overturned wine bottle, the two men **react** in sharp contrast.
*   **💡 亮点：** `Staring at...` 非谓语动词作状语，用得非常标准！
*   **批改：** **完美！** 这一句干净利落。

#### **第三句（挖细节·警报再次拉响 🚨）**
> **你的原文：** With an air of anxiety, the man on the left **sighs** that "It is all finished," whereas the fellow on the right **simle** contently, **rejoicing** that "fortunately, there is still some left."
*   **💡 亮点：** `sighs` (叹气) 和 `rejoicing` (欢庆/庆幸) 用词很准。
*   **致命错误（主谓一致 + 拼写）：**
    *   你写的是 `the fellow on the right simle`。
    *   **错误 1 (拼写)：** `simle` ❌ -> `smile` ✅
    *   **错误 2 (三单)：** `fellow` 是单数（一个人），谓语动词必须加 **s**！
    *   🔴 `fellow ... simle` -> 🟢 `fellow ... smiles`
    *   *大师痛心疾首：* 上一篇是 `boy are`，这一篇是 `fellow smile`。这就是你目前最大的扣分隐患！考官看到这种错误，会认为你基础语法不扎实。
*   **小建议：** 引号内的句子首字母通常大写，你做得对。

### 📝 最终修正示范（背诵这个无瑕疵版本）

> Laid out in front of us is a **thought-provoking** cartoon concerning two different attitudes towards the same **setback**. **Staring at** the overturned wine bottle, the two men **react** in sharp contrast. **With an air of anxiety**, the man on the left **sighs** that "It is all finished," whereas the fellow on the right **smiles** contently, **rejoicing** that "fortunately, there is still some left."
