### 🔍 逐句像素级欣赏

#### **第一句（总起句）**
> **你的原文：** **Undoubtedly**, what has been mirrored from the drawing is a fact that **putting plans into practice** should be given top priority.
*   **💡 亮点 1：** `Undoubtedly` 终于拼对了！🎉
*   **💡 亮点 2：** `putting plans into practice` (将计划付诸实践)，这个短语用得太精准了！完美切合 2017 年“空谈 vs 实干”的主题。
*   **批改：** 完美无瑕。

#### **第二句（正面论证）**
> **你的原文：** Rationally, we are **supposed to** take actions, as diligence transforms persistence into excellence, enabling our reading plans to yield extraordinary results.
*   **💡 亮点 1：** `supposed to` 有 **d** 了！感动！😭
*   **💡 亮点 2：** `enabling... to yield...` 这个结构再次出现，说明你已经完全掌握了这个长难句模型。
*   **小建议：** `take actions` 没错，但在表示抽象的“采取行动”时，`take action` (不可数) 更常见一点点，不过你的写法完全没问题。

#### **第三句（反面论证/升华）**
> **你的原文：** However, some ~~fails~~ **fail** to appreciate this; laziness kills their initiative, and they are satisfied with merely "owning" books, which can prevent potential from being realized, and **leave their minds empty**.
*   **🟠 唯一的遗憾（涂改）：** 你写了 `some fails`，然后立刻意识到 `some` 是复数，改成了 `fail`。
    *   **表扬：** 说明你的**自我纠错机制**已经启动了！你对语法的敏感度提高了。
    *   **批评：** 下次要把这个纠错过程放在**脑子里**，而不是**纸上**。
*   **💡 高光时刻：**
    *   `prevent potential from being realized` (阻碍潜力的实现) —— **太高级了！** 被动语态用在不定式里，满分！
    *   `leave their minds empty` (让大脑空空如也) —— **神来之笔！** 直接回扣主题（买书不读=脑子空空），非常深刻！

### 📝 最终示范文本（这篇就是范文！）

> **Undoubtedly**, what has been mirrored from the drawing is a fact that **putting plans into practice** should be given top priority. Rationally, we are **supposed to** take **action**, as diligence transforms persistence into excellence, enabling our reading plans to yield extraordinary results. However, some **fail** to appreciate this; laziness kills their initiative, and they are satisfied with merely "owning" books, which can **prevent potential from being realized** and **leave their minds empty**.
