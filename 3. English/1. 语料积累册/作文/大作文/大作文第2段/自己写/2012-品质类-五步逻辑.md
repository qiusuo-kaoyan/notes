### 🔍 逐句像素级诊断

#### **第一句（总起句）**
> **你的原文：** **Undoubkly**, **Undoubtely**, **Undoubtedly**, what has been mirrored from the drawing is a fact **the** that a positive attitude **should been given** top priority.
*   **🔴 卷面灾难：** 开头连续涂改两次。
    *   *大师建议：* 实在不会拼 `Undoubtedly`，就写 `Without a doubt` 或者 `Clearly`。
*   **🔴 致命语法错误：** `should been given` ❌
    *   *语法分析：* 情态动词 (should/can/must) + **be** (原形) + Done (过去分词)。绝对不能加 been！
    *   🟢 **修正：** `should be given` ✅
*   **🟠 赘词：** `a fact the that`，划掉了 `the`，还是卷面问题。

#### **第二句（正面论证）**
> **你的原文：** Rationally, we are supposed to **cultivate** optimism, which **equips ind- -ividuals** to thrive in **uncer uncertainty** , turning change into strength.
*   **💡 亮点：** `turning change into strength`（化改变为力量），这个非谓语动词做结果状语，写得太漂亮了！
*   **🔴 拼写/卷面：**
    *   `ind- -ividuals`：换行断词要小心，而且你划掉了横杠，看起来很脏。
    *   `uncer uncertainty`：又是写了一半涂改。想好了再下笔！

#### **第三句（反面论证/对比）**
> **你的原文：** However, some fail to maintain hope: they lose heart and allow difficulties to easily overwhelm them, which can leave individuals fragile in change**. and** doom them to failure.
*   **💡 亮点：** `lose heart` (灰心) 和 `overwhelm` (压倒) 用词很地道。
*   **🔴 致命句法错误（断句）：** `...fragile in change. And doom them to failure.`
    *   *语法分析：* `And doom them to failure` 没有主语，不能独立成句！它应该和前面的 `leave` 并列，共用 `which can` 这个主语。
    *   🟢 **修正：** 把句号改成逗号，或者直接去掉句号。 `...which can leave individuals fragile in change and doom them to failure.`

### 📝 最终修正示范（整洁版）

请对比你的手写稿，看看干净的卷面是多么重要：

> **Undoubtedly**, what has been mirrored from the drawing is a fact that a positive attitude **should be given** top priority. Rationally, we are supposed to **cultivate** optimism, which **equips** individuals to thrive in uncertainty, **turning** change into strength. However, some fail to maintain hope: they lose heart and allow difficulties to easily **overwhelm** them, which can leave individuals fragile in change **and doom** them to failure.
