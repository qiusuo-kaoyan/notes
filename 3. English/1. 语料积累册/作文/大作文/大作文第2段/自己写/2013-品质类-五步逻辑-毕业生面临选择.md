### 🔍 逐句像素级诊断

#### **第一句（总起句）**
> **你的原文：** **Undoubtedly**, what has been mirrored from the drawing is a fact that independence and responsibility **should be given** top priority.
*   **💡 进步：** `Undoubtedly` 拼对了！`should be given` 用对了！这一句无可挑剔，完美！👏
*   **批改：** 满分起手。

#### **第二句（正面论证）**
> **你的原文：** Rationally, we are **suppose** to ~~cultivate~~ be responsible for our future, as responsibility links **autonomy** with outcomes.
*   **🔴 语法致命伤：** `we are suppose to` ❌ -> **`we are supposed to`** ✅
    *   *大师咆哮：* `be supposed to` (应该/被期望做某事) 是固定搭配，**一定要加 d！** 就像 `used to` 一样，这是阅卷老师的“敏感点”。
*   **💡 亮点：** `links autonomy with outcomes` (将自主权与结果挂钩)，这个表达非常有深度，体现了你的逻辑思考能力。
*   **卷面：** 划掉 `cultivate` 略显杂乱，下次思考清楚再写。

#### **第三句（反面论证/后果）**
> **你的原文：** However, some fail to make their own **desicion**; they ~~fl~~ follow the herd and laziness kills their initiative, which can leave individuals fragile in change, unable to **complete**. in the job market.
*   **🔴 拼写错误：** `desicion` ❌ -> **`decision`** ✅
    *   *记忆口诀：* 决定 (decide) -> 名词 (decision)。中间是 s-i-o-n，不是 c。
*   **💡 亮点：** `follow the herd` (随大流) 用得太地道了！
*   **🔴 词汇致命伤：** `complete` (完成) ❌ -> **`compete`** (竞争) ✅
    *   *语境分析：* 你想说“无法在就业市场中**竞争**”，而不是“无法**完成**”。少了一个 `l`，意思完全变了！
*   **标点/卷面：** `complete.` 后面有个点，而且 `fragile in change` 后面最好用逗号，引出 `unable to...` 做结果状语。

### 📝 最终修正示范（背诵这个无瑕疵版）

请重点看我**加粗**和<u>下划线</u>的地方：

> **Undoubtedly**, what has been mirrored from the drawing is a fact that independence and responsibility **should be given** top priority. Rationally, we are **supposed to** be responsible for our future, as responsibility links autonomy with outcomes. However, some fail to make their own **decisions**; they follow the herd and laziness kills their initiative, which can leave individuals fragile in change, unable to **compete** in the job market.
