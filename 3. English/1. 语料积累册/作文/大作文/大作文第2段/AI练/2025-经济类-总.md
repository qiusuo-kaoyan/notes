### 🛠️ 实战组装：2025 年“家电拥有量”

**1. 锁定模板：** 经济类通用模板（Rooted in...）
**2. 锁定语料：**
*   **主题词：** `the popularity of home appliances` (家电的普及) / `durable consumer goods` (耐用消费品)
*   **核心原因 1（钱多了）：** `national economic strength` (国家经济实力) -> `purchasing power` (购买力)
*   **核心原因 2（特定语料）：** 你的语料包里有一句神句：`More home devices signal upgraded living standards.` (家电增多昭示生活水准升级)。
*   **核心原因 3（需求变了）：** `content with mere subsistence` (满足温饱) -> `comfort and convenience` (舒适与便利)。

**【第二段·满分拼装版】**

**Step 1: 开头点题（套模板）**
**Rooted in everyday life, it is necessary to examine in depth the factors behind** the rapid growth of these figures.
*(立足日常生活，有必要深入探讨这些数字快速增长背后的因素。)*

**Step 2: 经济基石（套模板）**
**There is no doubt that as national economic strength continues to rise, it has directly brought** people's **purchasing power** **ever greater attention.**
*(毫无疑问，随着国家经济实力不断提升，直接使得人们的**购买力**受到越来越多的关注/提升。)*
*注：把模板里的“题目相关词”换成 `people's purchasing power`，逻辑通顺。*

**Step 3: 语料植入（这是高分关键！直接抄你的语料包）**
**In addition,** **more home devices signal upgraded living standards**, **which** **naturally leads people to pursue** a more efficient lifestyle.
*(此外，**家电增多昭示生活水准升级**，这自然引导人们追求**更高效的生活方式**。)*
*注：这里稍微改了一下模板的 `online media`，直接用了你资料里的“家电专用句”，完美契合！*

**Step 4: 需求升级（套模板）**
**Equally important, as people are no longer content with mere subsistence but place greater emphasis on** **comfort and convenience**, **these appliances** **happen to meet everyday needs.**
*(同样重要的是，当人们不再仅满足于温饱，而是更注重**舒适与便利**时，**这些家电**正好能够满足人们日常生活中的需求。)*
*注：把模板里的 `emotional fulfillment` (情绪价值) 改成 `comfort and convenience` (舒适便利)，更贴切家电的主题。*

---

### 🚀 最终成文（背诵这个段落！）

> **Rooted in everyday life, it is necessary to examine in depth the factors behind the rapid growth of these figures. There is no doubt that as national economic strength continues to rise, it has directly brought people's purchasing power ever greater attention. In addition, more home devices signal upgraded living standards, which naturally leads people to pursue a more efficient lifestyle. Equally important, as people are no longer content with mere subsistence but place greater emphasis on comfort and convenience, these appliances happen to meet everyday needs.**
