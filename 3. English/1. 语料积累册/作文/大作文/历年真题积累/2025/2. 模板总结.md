##### 1. 黄皮书模板(经济类万能第二段)
**过渡句: 趋势归功于因素**
> This upward trend can be attributed to several factors.

**1. 国家层面--经济增长(居民收入增加, 购买力增强)**
> In the first place, thanks to its economic growth, China has witnessed rises in household incomes and purchasing power.

**2. 社会层面--科技创新(降低生产成本, 使得产品更加实惠)**
> Second, technological innovations have played a crucial role in reducing cost of production, which in turn have made domestic appliances more affordable.

**3. 政府层面--政策刺激(鼓励消费, 助力中低收入水平家庭)**
> Additionally, government policies that encourage spending, such as subsidies and tax breaks, have stimulated demands for durable goods, making them more accessible to low and middle income families.
