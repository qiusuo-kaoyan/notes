虽然是一道科技题，但我们完全可以用**【品质类语料包】**来降维打击！

*   **为什么大家只看手机不理人？**
    *   本质是**“自私（Selfishness）”**：只顾自己爽（personal entertainment），不顾聚会氛围（collective well-being）。
    *   表现是**“冷漠（Indifference）”**：切断了与身边人的联系（weakens social bonds）。

### 📝 2015 年英语一真题《手机时代的聚会》· 满分拼装实战

#### **第一段：图画描述（3 句式·讽刺现状版）**

> **Laid out in front of us is a satirical cartoon concerning** the detrimental effects of mobile phone addiction. **Sitting around a dinner table**, four friends are absorbed in their smartphones instead of communicating with each other. **With an air of indifference**, they ignore the delicious food and their companions, implying that technology, while connecting the world, creates distance between people nearby.

*   **大师解析：**
    *   **句 1（总）：** `Laid out... concerning...` （主题：手机依赖的危害 `detrimental effects of mobile phone addiction`）。
    *   **句 2（分）：** `Sitting around...` （非谓语开头）。描述核心动作：沉迷手机（absorbed in...），不交流（instead of communicating）。
    *   **句 3（细）：** `With an air of indifference...` （**复用语料！**）。神态必须是“冷漠”。最后 `implying` 升华一下：科技拉近了世界，却疏远了身边人（classic sentence）。

---

#### **第二段：核心论证（五步逻辑法 + 自私/冷漠语料）**

> **Undoubtedly, what has been mirrored from the drawing is a fact that** **rational use of technology** **should be given top priority.** **Rationally, we are supposed to** cherish face-to-face opportunities, as **real interaction builds trust and intimacy**. **However, some, intentionally or unintentionally, fail to** resist the temptation of digital devices; **selfishness narrows their vision, placing personal entertainment above collective communication**. **which can** **weaken social bonds and isolate individuals from real life**.

*   **大师解析（全程高能复用）：**
    *   **点题：** 填入 `rational use of technology`（理性使用科技）。
    *   **正面论证（铺垫）：** 既然是反面现象，正面就简单说：我们要珍惜面对面（cherish face-to-face opportunities）。
    *   **反面论证（自私）：** **直接抄语料包第 9 页（自私）！**
        *   原句：`placing personal gain above collective well-being`.
        *   微调：`placing personal entertainment above collective communication`. （把“私利”改成“个人娱乐”，把“集体利益”改成“集体交流”）。这招简直绝了！
    *   **反面后果（冷漠）：** **直接抄语料包第 9 页（冷漠）！**
        *   原句：`Indifference weakens social bonds, isolating vulnerable groups`.
        *   微调：去掉 `vulnerable groups`，改为 `isolate individuals from real life`（使个人与现实生活隔绝）。完美扣题！

---

#### **第三段：归纳建议（思维转变模板）**

> **To wrap it all up, the path to** **genuine human connection** **should be approached by a shift in mindset: translating our focus on** "**digital indulgence**" **into that on** "**real-world interaction**". **Such a move may hold a particular promise of** **a harmonious and connected society**.

*   **大师解析：**
    *   **目标：** `genuine human connection`（真正的人际连接）。
    *   **旧观念：** `digital indulgence`（数字沉迷）—— 对应“玩手机”。
    *   **新观念：** `real-world interaction`（现实互动）—— 对应“聊天”。
    *   **美好未来：** `a harmonious and connected society`（和谐连通的社会）。

### 💡 最终整篇范文（180 词左右）

**Laid out in front of us is a satirical cartoon concerning** the detrimental effects of mobile phone addiction. **Sitting around a dinner table**, four friends are absorbed in their smartphones instead of communicating with each other. **With an air of indifference**, they ignore the delicious food and their companions, implying that technology, while connecting the world, creates distance between people nearby.

**Undoubtedly, what has been mirrored from the drawing is a fact that** rational use of technology **should be given top priority.** **Rationally, we are supposed to** cherish face-to-face opportunities, as real interaction builds trust and intimacy. **However, some, intentionally or unintentionally, fail to** resist the temptation of digital devices; **selfishness narrows their vision, placing personal entertainment above collective communication**. **which can** weaken social bonds and isolate individuals from real life.

**To wrap it all up, the path to** genuine human connection **should be approached by a shift in mindset: translating our focus on** "digital indulgence" **into that on** "real-world interaction". **Such a move may hold a particular promise of** a harmonious and connected society.
