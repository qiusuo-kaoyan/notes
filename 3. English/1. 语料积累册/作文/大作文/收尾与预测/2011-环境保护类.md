### 📝 2011 年英语一真题《旅途之余》· 满分拼装实战

#### **第一段：图画描述（调用【3 句式】笔记）**

> **Laid out in front of us is a satirical cartoon concerning** uncivilized behavior in tourism. **Cruising on a boat**, a group of tourists are throwing leftovers and garbage directly into the river. **With an air of indifference**, they turn a blind eye to the floating waste, implying that their temporary convenience comes at the cost of the environment.

*   **大师解析：**
    *   **句 1（总）：** `Laid out... concerning...` （倒装句，定调为讽刺 `satirical`）。
    *   **句 2（分）：** `Cruising on a boat...` （非谓语动词 Doing 开头，替换了 sitting/standing）。
    *   **句 3（细）：** `With an air of indifference...` （冷漠的神情，直接复用 2022 年讲座题的语料）。

---

#### **第二段：核心论证（调用【五步逻辑法】+【品质语料包】）**

> **Undoubtedly, what has been mirrored from the drawing is a fact that** **environmental awareness** **should be given top priority.** **Rationally, we are supposed to** protect nature, as **responsibility links autonomy with outcomes** and keeps our behavior accountable. **As a result, we can** foster **sustainable development** and create a harmonious living environment. **However, some, intentionally or unintentionally, fail to** behave properly; **selfishness narrows their vision, placing personal convenience above collective well-being**. **which can** **burden nature** and lead to **tension between man and nature**.

*   **大师解析（全程高能复用）：**
    *   **点题：** 填入 `environmental awareness`（环保意识）。
    *   **正面论证（责任）：** 直接抄语料包第 7 页（责任感）—— `responsibility links autonomy with outcomes`。意为：我们要对自己的行为后果负责（不能乱扔）。
    *   **正面结果（可持续）：** 借用经济/旅游类的 `sustainable development`。
    *   **反面论证（自私）：** 直接抄语料包第 9 页（自私）—— `Selfishness narrows vision...`。只需把 `gain`（利益）微调为 `convenience`（便利），精准打击乱扔垃圾这种图方便的行为。
    *   **反面后果（负担）：** 借用语料包第 9 页（逃避责任）—— `burden others` 改为 `burden nature`（给大自然增加负担）。

---

#### **第三段：归纳建议（调用【思维转变】模板）**

> **To wrap it all up, the path to** **an eco-friendly society** **should be approached by a shift in mindset: translating our focus on** "**selfishness**" **into that on** "**public morality**". **Such a move may hold a particular promise of** **a harmonious coexistence between nature and human beings**.

*   **大师解析：**
    *   **目标：** `an eco-friendly society`（环境友好型社会）。
    *   **旧观念：** `selfishness`（自私）—— 呼应第二段。
    *   **新观念：** `public morality`（公德）—— 呼应主题。
    *   **美好未来：** `harmonious coexistence`（和谐共存）—— 升华。

---

### 💡 最终整篇范文（180 词左右，完美字数）

**Laid out in front of us is a satirical cartoon concerning** uncivilized behavior in tourism. **Cruising on a boat**, a group of tourists are throwing leftovers and garbage directly into the river. **With an air of indifference**, they turn a blind eye to the floating waste, implying that their temporary convenience comes at the cost of the environment.

**Undoubtedly, what has been mirrored from the drawing is a fact that** environmental awareness **should be given top priority.** **Rationally, we are supposed to** protect nature, as **responsibility links autonomy with outcomes** and keeps our behavior accountable. **As a result, we can** foster sustainable development and create a harmonious living environment. **However, some, intentionally or unintentionally, fail to** behave properly; **selfishness narrows their vision, placing personal convenience above collective well-being**. **which can** burden nature and lead to tension between man and nature.

**To wrap it all up, the path to** an eco-friendly society **should be approached by a shift in mindset: translating our focus on** "selfishness" **into that on** "public morality". **Such a move may hold a particular promise of** a harmonious coexistence between nature and human beings.
