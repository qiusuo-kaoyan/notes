### 📝 2019 年真题（支教项目咨询）· 满分拼装实战

#### **第一段：开门见山（表明身份+回复目的）**

> **Dear Friend,**
>
> **I hope this letter finds you well! I am Li Ming,** a member of the "Aiding Rural Primary Schools" project. **I am writing for the purpose of** providing you with essential information regarding the project **in a clear and timely manner**.

*   **大师解析：**
    *   **句 1：** 万能问候。
    *   **句 2：** 身份介绍（抄题目：working for the project...）。
    *   **句 3：** **复用语料包 Page 5（第二句挑选-2）**：`The aim of this message is to provide you with essential information regarding...`。稍微改写成 `I am writing for the purpose of...`，完全一致。

---

#### **第二段：核心介绍（复用“活动描述”骨架）**

这里我们需要把“支教项目”当成一个“长期活动”来写。

> **The following details are presented for your consideration.**
>
> **First and foremost, starting from** next month, **this meaningful project is to be carried out** in a remote village in western China.
>
> **In addition, what is notable is that** the project **aims to** improve the educational conditions of rural children and **broaden their horizons**.
>
> **By the way, it involves a wide range of activities, including** teaching English, organizing sports games, and **art appreciation**. **As an international volunteer, your presence will undoubtedly add luster to the event.**

*   **大师解析（拼装艺术）：**
    *   **总起句：** 万能总起。
    *   **时间地点：** **复用“活动骨架”**：`Starting from... is to be carried out...` （把 hosted 换成 carried out，更适合项目）。
    *   **目的：** **复用“活动骨架”**：`aims to... broaden their horizons`（开阔视野，语料包原词）。
    *   **内容：** **复用“活动骨架”**：`involves... including... art appreciation`（艺术鉴赏，语料包原词）。
    *   **特供金句：** **复用“邀请信”金句**：`As an international volunteer, your presence will undoubtedly add luster to the event.` （留学生来支教，绝对是蓬荜生辉，这句用得非常地道！）

---

#### **第三段：礼貌收尾（复用 Page 9 句型）**

> **If you have any further questions, please feel free to contact me.** **I am looking forward to your** active participation.
>
> **Yours sincerely,**
> **Li Ming**

*   **大师解析：**
    *   **句 1：** 标准客套（有这类回复信/通知信专用）。
    *   **句 2：** **复用语料包 Page 9**：`looking forward to your participation`。

### 💡 完整范文（100% 语料库拼装版）

**Dear Friend,**

**I hope this letter finds you well! I am Li Ming,** a member of the "Aiding Rural Primary Schools" project. **I am writing for the purpose of** providing you with essential information regarding the project **in a clear and timely manner**.

**The following details are presented for your consideration.** **First and foremost, starting from** next month, **this meaningful project is to be carried out** in a remote village in western China. **In addition, what is notable is that** the project **aims to** improve the educational conditions of rural children and **broaden their horizons**. **By the way, it involves a wide range of activities, including** teaching English, organizing sports games, and **art appreciation**. **As an international volunteer, your presence will undoubtedly add luster to the event.**

**If you have any further questions, please feel free to contact me.** **I am looking forward to your** active participation.

**Yours sincerely,**
**Li Ming**

---

### 🎓 大师复盘：

**这道题证明了什么？**
1.  **“活动描述”骨架** 不仅能写“通知”，也能写“回复咨询信”。只要是让你介绍一个事情的细节，这套骨架（Start -> Aim -> Content）就是通用的。
2.  **“Add luster（增光添彩）”** 这句话不仅能用来“拍专家马屁”，也能用来“鼓励志愿者”。语料的灵活运用是高分的关键。

**现在，小作文的几大门派：**
*   **建议/推荐信**（First and foremost, doing... is advisable...）
*   **通知/邀请信**（Starting from... aims to...）
*   **咨询回复/介绍信**（同上）

你都已经掌握了对应的**“万能积木”**。
小作文不需要太多的文采，**格式对、要点全、句子没有语法错误**，就是高分。
