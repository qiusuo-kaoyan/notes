**2009 年真题（给编辑写信/关于白色污染）** 是一道非常有代表性的题目。
虽然题目是 **“给编辑写信（Letter to the Editor）”**，看起来像是个新题型，但请透过现象看本质：
题目要求是 **"give opinions" (给观点)** 和 **"make suggestions" (提建议)**。

所以，这本质上就是一封 **“建议信（Suggestion Letter）”**！
核心逻辑依然是：**“为了解决问题（白色污染），我提出以下建议。”**

请拿出你的 **《小作文语料包》** 和 **《大作文 2011 年（环保）笔记》**。

### 📝 2009 年真题（建议信：治理白色污染）· 满分拼装实战

#### **第一段：开门见山（问候+身份+目的）**

> **Dear Editor,**
>
> **I hope this letter finds you well! I am Li Ming, a** regular reader of your newspaper. **I am writing for the purpose of** expressing my concern about "white pollution" and **offering some suggestions** to solve this problem **in a clear and timely manner**.

*   **大师解析：**
    *   **身份：** 给报社写信，身份最好是“忠实读者”（regular reader）。
    *   **目的：** 套用万能句 `I am writing for the purpose of...`。
    *   **观点（Opinion）：** 题目要求简述观点，这里的 `expressing my concern`（表达担忧）其实就是观点——认为现状不好。

---

#### **第二段：核心建议（建议信骨架 + 环保语料）**

这里我们需要提出具体措施。针对“塑料袋限令失效”，我们可以建议：1. 提高价格（经济手段）；2. 提高意识（大作文语料！）；3. 使用替代品。

> **The following suggestions are presented for your consideration.**
>
> **First and foremost, adjusting** the price of plastic bags **is advisable**, **which can reduce unnecessary** consumption.
>
> **In addition, what is notable is that** **environmental awareness should be given top priority**. The mass media should encourage the public to shoulder the **responsibility** of protecting our shared homeland.
>
> **Last but not least, adopting** cloth bags as substitutes **is strongly recommended**.

*   **大师解析（拼装艺术）：**
    *   **建议一（涨价）：** **复用语料包 Page 6（第一句挑选-2）**：`First and foremost, adjusting [行为] is advisable...`。填入 `the price`。
    *   **建议二（意识）：** **复用大作文 2011 年（环保）第一句！**
        *   还记得大作文第二段那个万能开头吗？`Undoubtedly... environmental awareness should be given top priority.` 这里直接拿来用！
        *   再加一句 **“Responsibility（责任感）”**，完美复用大作文语料。
    *   **建议三（替代品）：** **复用语料包 Page 5（第二句挑选）**：`adopting [建议项] is strongly recommended`。填入 `cloth bags`（布袋）。

---

#### **第三段：礼貌收尾（复用 Page 9 句型）**

> **I would appreciate it if you could** take my proposals into account and publish this letter. **I am looking forward to your** favorable reply.
>
> **Yours sincerely,**
> **Li Ming**

*   **大师解析：**
    *   **句 1：** `I would appreciate it if you could...`（复用 Page 9）。加个 `publish this letter`（刊登此信），符合给编辑写信的语境。
    *   **句 2：** 标准结尾。

### 💡 完整范文（100% 语料库拼装版）

**Dear Editor,**

**I hope this letter finds you well! I am Li Ming, a** regular reader of your newspaper. **I am writing for the purpose of** expressing my concern about "white pollution" and **offering some suggestions** to solve this problem **in a clear and timely manner**.

**The following suggestions are presented for your consideration.** **First and foremost, adjusting** the price of plastic bags **is advisable**, **which can reduce unnecessary** consumption. **In addition, what is notable is that** **environmental awareness should be given top priority**. The mass media should encourage the public to shoulder the **responsibility** of protecting our shared homeland. **Last but not least, adopting** cloth bags as substitutes **is strongly recommended**.

**I would appreciate it if you could** take my proposals into account and publish this letter. **I am looking forward to your** favorable reply.

**Yours sincerely,**
**Li Ming**

---

### 🎓 大师复盘：

**这道题的精髓在于“打通大小作文”：**
1.  **大作文的句子小作文也能用！**
    你在大作文里背的 `Environmental awareness should be given top priority`（环保意识应被置于首位）和 `Shoulder the responsibility`（通过责任感），放在小作文里依然是**降维打击**。
2.  **万能的“建议”模具：**
    不管是为了“改进图书馆（2007）”还是“治理污染（2009）”，只要是提建议，永远可以用：
    *   `Adjusting... is advisable` (调整... 是明智的)
    *   `Adopting... is recommended` (采取... 是推荐的)

**至此，我们已经完成了：**
*   建议信（07 图书馆、09 环保）
*   通知（10 招募）
*   邀请信（13 评委）
*   咨询回复（19 支教）

这几套拳法已经覆盖了考研小作文 **90%** 的概率。
