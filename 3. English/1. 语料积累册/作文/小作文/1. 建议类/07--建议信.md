### 📝 2007 年真题（建议信：改进图书馆服务）· 满分拼装实战

#### **第一段：开门见山（问候+身份+目的）**

> **Dear Sir or Madam,**
>
> **I hope this letter finds you well! I am Li Ming, a** student who is a frequent visitor to our library. **I am writing for the purpose of** offering some suggestions for improving the service of our library **in a clear and timely manner**.

*   **大师解析：**
    *   **句 1：** 万能问候。
    *   **句 2：** 身份介绍（Li Ming, a student...）。
    *   **句 3：** **直接复用语料包 Page 5（第二句挑选-1）**：`I am writing for the purpose of addressing [目的] in a clear and timely manner.` 我把 `addressing` 换成了 `offering suggestions`，后面直接抄！

---

#### **第二段：核心建议（复用 Page 6 & 7 句型）**

这里我们需要提出两条具体建议：1. 更新书籍；2. 改善环境。

> **The following suggestions are presented for your consideration.**
>
> **First and foremost, updating** the collection of books **is advisable**, **which can** help students **gain firsthand insight into** the latest academic trends.
>
> **In addition, it would be wise to** improve the reading environment, **so as to** create a quiet atmosphere for us.

*   **大师解析（拼装艺术）：**
    *   **总起句：** 咱们之前总结的万能总起。
    *   **建议一：** **直接复用语料包 Page 6（第一句挑选-2）**！
        *   原句：`First and foremost, adjusting [行为] is advisable, which can reduce unnecessary trouble...`
        *   *改装：* 把 `adjusting` 换成 `updating`（更新书籍）。后果部分借用了 **Page 6（第一句挑选-4）** 的 `gain firsthand insight into`（获得第一手洞察），显得非常学术！
    *   **建议二：** **直接复用语料包 Page 7（第三句挑选-4）**！
        *   原句：`In addition, it would be wise to adjust your plan so as to...`
        *   *改装：* 把 `adjust your plan` 换成 `improve the reading environment`。

---

#### **第三段：礼貌收尾（复用 Page 9 句型）**

> **I would appreciate it if you could** take my proposals into account. **I am looking forward to your** favorable reply.
>
> **Yours sincerely,**
> **Li Ming**

*   **大师解析：**
    *   **直接复用语料包 Page 9（第二句）**：`I would appreciate it if you could [干啥].`
    *   填入：`take my proposals into account`（考虑我的建议）。

---

### 💡 完整范文（100% 语料库拼装版）

**Dear Sir or Madam,**

**I hope this letter finds you well! I am Li Ming, a** student who is a frequent visitor to our library. **I am writing for the purpose of** offering some suggestions for improving the service of our library **in a clear and timely manner**.

**The following suggestions are presented for your consideration.** **First and foremost, updating** the collection of books **is advisable**, **which can** help students **gain firsthand insight into** the latest academic trends. **In addition, it would be wise to** improve the reading environment, **so as to** create a quiet atmosphere for us.

**I would appreciate it if you could** take my proposals into account. **I am looking forward to your** favorable reply.

**Yours sincerely,**
**Li Ming**

---

### 🎓 大师复盘：

你看，写这篇作文，我只动脑子想了这几个短语：
1.  **offering suggestions** (提建议)
2.  **updating the collection of books** (更新藏书)
3.  **latest academic trends** (最新学术动态)
4.  **improve the reading environment** (改善环境)

**剩下的所有部分（占了 80%的字数），全是从你的语料包图片里抄下来的！**
比如 `gain firsthand insight into` 这种高级表达，原本是用来写“邀请信”的，被我们拿来写“图书馆建议”，毫无违和感，反而加分！

**这就是“黑猫白猫论”的完美应用。**
