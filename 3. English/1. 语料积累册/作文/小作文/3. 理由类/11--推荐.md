### 📝 2011 年真题（推荐电影）· 满分拼装实战

#### **第一段：开门见山（问候+背景+目的）**

> **Dear Friend,**
>
> **I hope this letter finds you well! I feel extremely delighted to hear that** you are looking for a good movie to watch this weekend. **I am writing this letter to recommend one of my favorite movies, *Forrest Gump*, to you.**

*   **大师解析：**
    *   **句 1：** 万能问候。
    *   **句 2：** **复用语料包 Page 4（第二句挑选-背景）**：`I feel extremely delighted to hear that...`（听说...... 我很高兴）。这里假定朋友想看电影。
    *   **句 3：** 直奔主题：推荐《阿甘正传》。

---

#### **第二段：推荐理由（推荐信骨架 + 品质语料）**

这里我们要用 **“三点式”** 罗列理由：1. 剧情好；2. 精神好（大作文语料）；3. 功效好（小作文语料）。

> **The reasons for my recommendation are listed as follows.**
>
> **First and foremost, this movie is characterized by** its touching plot and profound theme, **which can** leave a deep impression on you.
>
> **In addition, what is notable is that** the hero's spirit **can be likened to** a beacon, inspiring us to face difficulties with **diligence and perseverance**.
>
> **Last but not least, watching this movie can** help you **relax and ease your mental pressure** after busy study.

*   **大师解析（拼装艺术）：**
    *   **理由一（剧情）：** **复用“介绍/通知”句型**：`...is characterized by...`（以...... 为特征）。填入 `touching plot`（感人剧情）。
    *   **理由二（精神）：** **大作文语料植入！**
        *   用了 **`can be likened to`**（可以比作...），这是你语料包 Page 49 的高级表达。
        *   用了 **`diligence and perseverance`**（勤奋与毅力），这是大作文 2020/2017 年的核心词汇！
        *   *含义：* 阿甘精神像灯塔，教我们勤奋坚持。这立意瞬间拉满！
    *   **理由三（功效）：** **复用小作文语料 Page 32**：`help you relax and ease your mental pressure`（帮你放松和缓解压力）。万能理由！

---

#### **第三段：礼貌收尾（期待反馈）**

> **I am convinced that you will enjoy it.** **I am looking forward to your** sharing your feelings with me.
>
> **Yours sincerely,**
> **Li Ming**

*   **大师解析：**
    *   **句 1：** `I am convinced that...`（我确信...）。
    *   **句 2：** 期待你看完跟我分享感受。

### 💡 完整范文（100% 语料库拼装版）

**Dear Friend,**

**I hope this letter finds you well! I feel extremely delighted to hear that** you are looking for a good movie to watch this weekend. **I am writing this letter to recommend one of my favorite movies, *Forrest Gump*, to you.**

**The reasons for my recommendation are listed as follows.** **First and foremost, this movie is characterized by** its touching plot and profound theme, **which can** leave a deep impression on you. **In addition, what is notable is that** the hero's spirit **can be likened to** a beacon, inspiring us to face difficulties with **diligence and perseverance**. **Last but not least, watching this movie can** help you **relax and ease your mental pressure** after busy study.

**I am convinced that you will enjoy it.** **I am looking forward to your** sharing your feelings with me.

**Yours sincerely,**
**Li Ming**
