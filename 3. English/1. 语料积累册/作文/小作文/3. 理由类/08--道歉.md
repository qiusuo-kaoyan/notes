**2008 年真题（还 CD/道歉信）** 是一道生活气息很浓的题目。
这就涉及到了小作文中非常重要的一类——**“道歉信（Letter of Apology）”**。

这类信件的逻辑闭环是：
1.  **认错：** 我错了（I am writing to apologize...）。
2.  **解释：** 为什么错（Explaining the reason...）。
3.  **补救：** 怎么办（Solution/Remediation）。
4.  **求谅解：** 请原谅（Appreciate your understanding）。

### 📝 2008 年真题（归还 CD/道歉信）· 满分拼装实战

#### **第一段：开门见山（问候+身份+道歉）**

> **Dear Bob,**
>
> **I hope this letter finds you well! I am Li Ming,** your former tenant. **I am writing for the purpose of** expressing my sincere regret for **taking your music CD by mistake**.

*   **大师解析：**
    *   **称呼：** 房东 Bob，算是熟人，直接 `Dear Bob,`。
    *   **句 1：** 万能问候。
    *   **句 2：** 身份介绍（former tenant 前房客）。
    *   **句 3：** **复用语料包 Page 7（第三段挑选-最后一词）**：`it is necessary for me to express my sincere regret for...`。这里稍微改写成 `I am writing for the purpose of expressing...`，更直接。

---

#### **第二段：解释+补救（复用 Page 6 & 8 句型）**

这里要写两个点：1. 为什么拿走了（解释：收拾行李时不小心）；2. 怎么还（补救：寄快递）。

> **The following details are presented for your consideration.**
>
> **First and foremost, explaining the reason behind** this error **is appropriate**, **which can help you understand the situation better**. I found the CD in my luggage when I was unpacking yesterday.
>
> **In addition, what is notable is that** I have already sent it back to you by express mail this morning.
>
> **Finally, I sincerely apologize for the inconvenience caused, and only through genuine remorse and active remediation can I obtain your understanding.**

*   **大师解析（拼装艺术）：**
    *   **总起句：** 万能总起。
    *   **解释原因：** **直接复用语料包 Page 6（第三句挑选-2）**！
        *   原句：`First and foremost, explaining the reason behind [事件] is appropriate, which can help you understand the situation better.`
        *   *填空：* 填入 `this error`（这个错误）。后面补一句大白话：昨天开箱子发现的。
    *   **提出补救：** **复用建议类句型**：`In addition, what is notable is that...`（值得注意的是... 我寄回去了）。
    *   **再次道歉（必杀技）：** **直接复用语料包 Page 8（倒数第二段）**！
        *   原句：`Finally, I sincerely apologize for the inconvenience caused, and only through genuine remorse and active remediation can I obtain your understanding.`
        *   *解析：* 这句话太高级了！“只有通过真诚的悔恨和积极的补救才能获得你的理解”。把这句话背下来，**所有道歉信通杀！**

---

#### **第三段：礼貌收尾（复用 Page 8 & 9 句型）**

> **Moreover, I appreciate your understanding while we deal with** this matter. **I am looking forward to your** favorable reply.
>
> **Yours sincerely,**
> **Li Ming**

*   **大师解析：**
    *   **句 1：** **直接复用语料包 Page 8（第一句）**：`Moreover, I appreciate your understanding while we deal with [道歉内容].`
    *   **句 2：** 标准结尾。

---

### 💡 完整范文（100% 语料库拼装版）

**Dear Bob,**

**I hope this letter finds you well! I am Li Ming,** your former tenant. **I am writing for the purpose of** expressing my sincere regret for **taking your music CD by mistake**.

**The following details are presented for your consideration.** **First and foremost, explaining the reason behind** this error **is appropriate**, **which can help you understand the situation better**. I found the CD in my luggage when I was unpacking yesterday. **In addition, what is notable is that** I have already sent it back to you by express mail this morning. **Finally, I sincerely apologize for the inconvenience caused, and only through genuine remorse and active remediation can I obtain your understanding.**

**Moreover, I appreciate your understanding while we deal with** this matter. **I am looking forward to your** favorable reply.

**Yours sincerely,**
**Li Ming**
