### 📝 2006 年真题（希望工程捐款）· 满分拼装实战

#### **第一段：开门见山（问候+身份+目的）**

> **Dear Sir or Madam,**
>
> **I hope this letter finds you well! I am Li Ming, a** university student who wishes to contribute to Project Hope. **I am writing for the purpose of** offering financial aid to a child in a remote area and **asking for your assistance** to find a candidate.

*   **大师解析：**
    *   **句 1：** 万能问候。
    *   **句 2：** 身份介绍（想捐款的大学生）。
    *   **句 3：** **复用语料包 Page 5**：`I am writing for the purpose of...`。把目的（offering aid / asking for assistance）填进去。

---

#### **第二段：核心细节（招募句型 + 勤奋语料）**

这里要写两个点：1. 想要什么样的孩子（标准）；2. 打算怎么资助（计划）。

> **The following details are presented for your consideration.**
>
> **First and foremost, regarding the candidate, the qualities I value most include** **diligence** and a strong desire for knowledge. **Previous experience** of dropping out due to poverty **is a priority**.
>
> **In addition, what is notable is that** my plan **aims to** help the child finish primary school education.
>
> **Last but not least, I plan to** provide 2,000 yuan annually to cover his or her tuition fees and living expenses.

*   **大师解析（拼装艺术）：**
    *   **总起句：** 万能总起。
    *   **找孩子（标准）：** **直接复用“招募通知”金句！**
        *   原句：`The qualities we value most include...`
        *   *填空：* **`diligence` (勤奋)** —— **复用大作文语料！** 加上 `desire for knowledge`（求知欲）。
        *   *补充：* `...is a priority`（... 是优先考虑的），改编自 `is a plus`。这里指“因贫辍学（dropping out due to poverty）”的孩子优先。
    *   **谈计划（目的）：** **复用“活动骨架”**：`aims to...`（旨在帮助孩子完成小学学业）。
    *   **谈细节（钱）：** 简单句 `I plan to provide...`。

---

#### **第三段：礼貌收尾（复用 Page 9 句型）**

> **I would appreciate it if you could** help me find a suitable child. **I am looking forward to your** favorable reply.
>
> **Yours sincerely,**
> **Li Ming**

*   **大师解析：**
    *   **句 1：** **复用语料包 Page 9**：`I would appreciate it if you could...`。
    *   **句 2：** 标准结尾。

---

### 💡 完整范文（100% 语料库拼装版）

**Dear Sir or Madam,**

**I hope this letter finds you well! I am Li Ming, a** university student who wishes to contribute to Project Hope. **I am writing for the purpose of** offering financial aid to a child in a remote area and **asking for your assistance** to find a candidate.

**The following details are presented for your consideration.** **First and foremost, regarding the candidate, the qualities I value most include** **diligence** and a strong desire for knowledge. **Previous experience** of dropping out due to poverty **is a priority**. **In addition, what is notable is that** my plan **aims to** help the child finish primary school education. **Last but not least, I plan to** provide 2,000 yuan annually to cover his or her tuition fees and living expenses.

**I would appreciate it if you could** help me find a suitable child. **I am looking forward to your** favorable reply.

**Yours sincerely,**
**Li Ming**

### 🎓 大师复盘：

**这道题再次证明了“语料复用”的威力：**
1.  **“招募通知”的句子也能用来“找人捐款”！**
    `The qualities we value most`（我们最看重的品质），不仅可以用来招志愿者，也可以用来描述你想资助的学生。这招**“移花接木”**非常高级。
2.  **大作文词汇永远的神！**
    **Diligence（勤奋）** 这个词，我们在大作文里用了，小作文里接着用。阅卷老师看到这个词，就知道你的词汇量是过关的。

**总结一下我们的小作文战绩：**
*   **建议/推荐信** —— 用 `is advisable / recommended`
*   **活动/介绍/咨询** —— 用 `Starting from... / aims to...`
*   **招募/找人** —— 用 `The qualities we value most...`
*   **邀请/客套** —— 用 `Add luster`

**这四把钥匙，已经能打开考研小作文所有的大门了！**
