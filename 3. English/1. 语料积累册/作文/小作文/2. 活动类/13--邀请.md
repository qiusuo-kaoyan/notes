**2013 年真题（邀请外教做评委）** 是一道非常标准的 **“邀请信（Invitation Letter）”**。

### 📝 2013 年真题（邀请信：英语演讲比赛）· 满分拼装实战

#### **第一段：开门见山（问候+身份+目的）**

> **Dear Professor,**
>
> **I hope this letter finds you well! I am Li Ming,** the organizer of the upcoming English speech contest. **I am writing this letter to extend a warm invitation to you to** serve as a judge for the final round of the contest.

*   **大师解析：**
    *   **句 1：** 万能问候。
    *   **句 2：** 身份介绍（organizer）。
    *   **句 3：** **复用语料包 Page 5（末尾句）**：`I am writing this letter to extend a warm invitation to you to...`（我写信是为了向您发出诚挚邀请...）。把 *volunteer* 换成 *serve as a judge*。

---

#### **第二段：核心细节（活动骨架 + 奉承金句）**

这里我们需要介绍比赛的时间地点，并说明为什么请他（因为他牛）。

> **The following arrangements are presented for your consideration.**
>
> **First and foremost, starting from** next Friday evening, **this grand contest is to be hosted** in the Student Center **on our campus**.
>
> **In addition, what is notable is that** with the theme of "Dreams and Passion", this event **aims to** improve students' oral English and **broaden their horizons**.
>
> **Last but not least, as a person of remarkable influence in** English education, **your presence will undoubtedly add luster to the event.**

*   **大师解析（拼装艺术）：**
    *   **总起句：** 依然用 `The following arrangements are presented...`。
    *   **时间地点：** **复用“活动骨架”**：`Starting from... is to be hosted...`。
    *   **目的/主题：** **复用“活动骨架”**：`aims to... broaden their horizons`（开阔视野，语料包原词）。顺便编了个主题 "Dreams and Passion"（梦想与激情）。
    *   **特供金句（必杀技）：** **直接复用“邀请信”金句**：
        *   原句：`As a person of remarkable influence in..., your presence will undoubtedly add luster to the event.`
        *   *填空：* 在 `in` 后面填 `English education`（英语教育）。
        *   *效果：* 这句话一出，外教看了心里美滋滋，绝对愿意来！

---

#### **第三段：礼貌收尾（复用 Page 9 句型）**

> **I would appreciate it if you could** accept my invitation. **I am looking forward to your** favorable reply.
>
> **Yours sincerely,**
> **Li Ming**

*   **大师解析：**
    *   **句 1：** **复用语料包 Page 9**：`I would appreciate it if you could...`。
    *   **句 2：** 标准结尾。

---

### 💡 完整范文（100% 语料库拼装版）

**Dear Professor,**

**I hope this letter finds you well! I am Li Ming,** the organizer of the upcoming English speech contest. **I am writing this letter to extend a warm invitation to you to** serve as a judge for the final round of the contest.

**The following arrangements are presented for your consideration.** **First and foremost, starting from** next Friday evening, **this grand contest is to be hosted** in the Student Center **on our campus**. **In addition, what is notable is that** with the theme of "Dreams and Passion", this event **aims to** improve students' oral English and **broaden their horizons**. **Last but not least, as a person of remarkable influence in** English education, **your presence will undoubtedly add luster to the event.**

**I would appreciate it if you could** accept my invitation. **I am looking forward to your** favorable reply.

**Yours sincerely,**
**Li Ming**

---

### 🎓 大师复盘：

**这道题再次印证了我们的策略：**
1.  **活动类通用模具：** `Starting from... is to be hosted... aims to...` 这一套，不仅能写“通知招募”，也能写“邀请信介绍背景”。
2.  **关键差异点：** 招募志愿者用 `qualities we value`（提要求），邀请评委用 `add luster`（拍马屁）。**只要记住了这一句的区别，这两种题型随意切换！**

到目前为止，你的小作文实战已经覆盖了：
*   **建议信**（2007 图书馆）
*   **通知**（2010 招募志愿者）
*   **咨询回复/介绍**（2019 支教项目）
*   **邀请信**（2013 邀请评委）

**这四篇作文，用的核心语料其实是同一套！** 这就是高效备考的真谛。

最后，提醒一下：如果是 **“推荐信（Recommendation）”**，逻辑也是一样的：
*   **开头：** I am writing to recommend [书/电影/人] to you.
*   **中间：** `First and foremost, [书名] is characterized by [特点].` + `In addition, reading it can broaden your horizons.` (你看，开阔视野这个词又是万能的！)
